# Sales Conversations Dashboard - Executive Summary

## Overview

**Sales Conversations Dashboard** is a unified platform that streamlines sales operations by bringing together all customer conversations, from initial contact through the entire customer lifecycle, in one intuitive interface.

---

## The Problem

Sales teams today struggle with:
- Scattered conversation data across multiple tools
- Poor visibility into customer journey progression
- Missed follow-ups leading to lost revenue
- Late detection of at-risk accounts
- Inefficient handoffs between pre-sales and post-sales teams

**Result:** Lost opportunities, reduced revenue, and poor customer experience.

---

## The Solution

A comprehensive dashboard with two integrated views:

### **Pre-Sales Dashboard**
Manage your pipeline from first contact to closed deal:
- Track all prospects and opportunities
- Monitor deal stages and progression
- Never miss a follow-up with built-in reminders
- View complete conversation history
- Filter by priority and status

### **Post-Sales Dashboard**
Ensure customer success and maximize lifetime value:
- Monitor account health in real-time
- Track support tickets and issues
- Manage renewals and prevent churn
- Identify upsell opportunities
- Proactive at-risk account alerts

### **Customer Journey Timeline**
Visualize the complete customer journey:
- Track every touchpoint from lead to active customer
- See activity distribution across phases
- Monitor key metrics (duration, activities, people, deal size)
- Identify successful patterns and bottlenecks
